Frequently asked questions

A few of the questions we hear from restaurant owners and managers when they consider adding online ordering.

Do I have to change my existing website?

No. We integrate online ordering into the website you already have using links and/or embedded experiences. Your web person only needs to add a button or link that we provide.

Will my staff need to learn a whole new system?

Staff see orders in a simple, consistent way through Square (or your existing POS integration, if available). We design the flow so it fits how your kitchen already works.

Who owns the Square account?

You do. We help set it up and manage the configuration, but the account and funds belong to your business. We can also work as an admin on an existing account.

How long does it take to go live?

For most restaurants, we can go from discovery to live ordering in **7–10 days**, depending on menu complexity and speed of approvals.
