Frequently asked questions

Short answers to what owners and managers usually ask before they start.

Do I have to change my existing website?

No. We integrate into the site you already have with clean “Order Online” buttons or links. Your web person just adds what we provide.

Will my staff need to learn a whole new system?

Orders show up in a simple, consistent way through Square (or your POS integration, if available). We design the flow around how your kitchen already works.

Who owns the Square account?

You own the account and funds. We help set things up and manage the configuration, typically as an admin on your existing Square account.

How long does it take to go live?

Most restaurants go from discovery to live ordering in 7–10 days, depending on menu complexity and how quickly we get approvals and assets.
